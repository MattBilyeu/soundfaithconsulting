ABOUT US

Tyson James, President

Tyson James has earned MAs in International Relations (American Military University) and Christian Apologetics (Biola University). Having operated as the Global Chapters Director and Director of Translations/Transcriptions for Reasonable Faith since 2017 to the present, he now seeks to employ his knowledge and experience to build the fields of Christian philosophy, theology, and apologetics. Tyson is a veteran of the US Air Force and currently lives in Las Vegas, NV with his wife, daughter, and son.

Our Mission

To glorify God by building the fields of Christian philosophy, theology, and apologetics through Christian consulting, talent management, literary representation, and public relations.
